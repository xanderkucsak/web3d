Controls: Arrow keys are used to drive. Tap W and S to control your speed, and use M and N to zoom in and out. If the zoom out function does not load the model, refresh the page and try again.

Artist Statement: This is a model that I have been working on as a part of a project to create a playable and explorable 3D map of the town of Blacksburg. During the production of the map I started to realize how many areas I recognize just by the roads such as main street and the drillfield. I was inspired to create an interface where you can drive around the map from above and explore the town. This model of blacksburg's roadways was constructed entirely from a single object by extruding every single road off of one main cube starting in the center of town. It consists of roughly 25,000 faces.
